Scrambled Eggs


Prep Time Cook Time Total Time
2 min 5 min 7 min
Jump To Nutrition Facts
4.9 (154 Reviews)
★ ★ ★ ★ ★

Ingredients:

  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 table spoon of butter (or oil)
  • 2 table spoon of milk (optional, for creaminess)
  • Salt, for taste
  • Black pepper, for taste
  • Fresh herbs (optional, e.g., chives or parsley)

    • Instructions:

    1. Crack and Whisk:

  • Crack the eggs into a bowl, add milk (if using), and whisk until well combined.

    • 2. Heat the Pan:

  • Melt butter in a non-stick skillet over medium-low heat.

    • 3. Cook the Eggs:

  • Pour in the eggs and let them sit for a few seconds before stirring.

    • 4. Gently Stir

  • Use a spatula to slowly push the eggs from the edges to the center, forming soft curds.

    • 5. Season

  • Add salt and pepper when the eggs are almost set.

    • 6. Finish Cooking

  • Remove from heat when slightly underdone (they’ll keep cooking from residual heat).

    • 7. Serve

  • Garnish with fresh herbs if desired and enjoy!
