Ingredients

For the crêpes:

  • 1 cup (120g) all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup (240ml) milk
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) water
  • 2 tbsp (30g) melted butter
  • 1 tbsp (12g) sugar (optional, for sweet crepes)
  • 1/2 tsp salt

    • For cooking:

  • Butter or oil for greasing the pan

    • Instructions

    1. Prepare the Batter:

  • In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar (if using), and salt.
  • In another bowl, whisk the eggs, milk, water, and melted butter until combined.
  • Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, whisking until smooth.
  • Let the batter rest for at least 15-30 minutes to remove air bubbles and allow the flour to absorb the liquid.

    • 2. Cook the Crepes:

  • Heat a nonstick skillet or crepe pan over medium heat. Lightly grease with butter or oil.
  • Pour about 1/4 cup of batter into the pan, swirling it to spread evenly.
  • Cook for about 1-2 minutes until the edges lift slightly and the bottom is golden brown.
  • Flip carefully and cook for another 30-60 seconds.
  • Transfer to a plate and repeat with the remaining batter.

    • 3. Transfer to a plate and repeat with the remaining batter:

  • Sweet: Serve with powdered sugar, fruit, chocolate spread, or whipped cream.
  • Savory: Fill with cheese, ham, eggs, or sautéed vegetables.
