Mango Lassi Recipe

Prep Time Cook Time Total Time

Ingredients

  • 1 cup ripe mango (peeled and chopped) or mango pulp
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt (Greek or regular)
  • 1/2 cup milk (or water for a lighter version)
  • 2 tbsp sugar or honey (adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder (optional, for authentic flavor)
  • 3-4 ice cubes (optional)
  • Saffron strands or chopped nuts (for garnish, optional)

    • Instructions

  • 1. In a blender, add chopped mango (or mango pulp), yogurt, milk, sugar, and cardamom powder.
  • 2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  • 3. Add ice cubes and blend again until well combined.
  • 4. Pour into a glass and garnish with saffron strands or chopped nuts if desired.
  • 5. Serve chilled and enjoy your refreshing Mango Lassi!
