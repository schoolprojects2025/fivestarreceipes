Chocolatte Mousse Recipe

Prep Time Cook Time Total Time
15 min 5 min 20 min (+ 2 hrs chilling time)
Jump To Nutrition Facts
4.6 (121 Reviews)
★ ★ ★ ★ ★

Ingredients

  • 6 ounces (170g) dark chocolate (60-70% cocoa), finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons (28g) unsalted butter
  • 2 large eggs, separated
  • 2 tablespoons (25g) granulated sugar
  • 1 cup (240ml) heavy whipping cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)
  • A pinch of salt

    • Instructions

    1.Melt the Chocolate

  • In a heatproof bowl, melt the chocolate and butter over a double boiler or in the microwave (in 20-second intervals, stirring in between).
  • Let it cool slightly.

    • 2.Whisk Egg Yolks & Sugar

  • In a separate bowl, whisk egg yolks and 1 tablespoon sugar until pale and creamy.
  • Stir into the melted chocolate mixture.

    • 3.Beat Egg Whites

  • In another clean bowl, whisk egg whites with a pinch of salt until soft peaks form.
  • Gradually add the remaining 1 tablespoon sugar, beating until stiff peaks form.

    • 4.Whip the Cream

  • In a separate bowl, whip heavy cream and vanilla until soft peaks form.

    • 5.Fold Everything Together

  • Gently fold the whipped cream into the chocolate mixture.
  • Then carefully fold in the beaten egg whites until smooth and airy.

    • .Chill & Serve

  • Divide into serving cups and refrigerate for at least 2 hours to set.
  • Garnish with whipped cream, shaved chocolate, or berries before serving.
