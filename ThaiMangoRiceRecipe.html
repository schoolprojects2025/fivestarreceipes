Thai Mango Rice Recipe

Prep Time Cook Time Total Time
5-7 min 10-12 min 15-20 min
Jump To Nutrition Facts
4.6 (121 Reviews)

Ingredients

For the sticky rice:

  • 1 cup glutinous (sticky) rice
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/4 tsp salt

    • For the coconut sauce:

  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp cornstarch (optional, for thickening)

    • For serving:

  • 1-2 ripe mangoes, peeled and sliced
  • 1 tbsp toasted sesame seeds or mung beans (optional)

    • Instructions

    1. Prepare the Sticky Rice:

  • Rinse the glutinous rice under cold water until the water runs clear. Soak in water for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  • Drain and steam the rice in a bamboo steamer (or use a heatproof dish inside a regular steamer) for 25-30 minutes until tender.

    • 2. Make the Sweet Coconut Rice:

  • While the rice is steaming, heat 1 cup coconut milk in a saucepan over low heat.
  • Add 1/4 cup sugar and 1/4 tsp salt, stirring until dissolved. Do not boil.
  • Once the rice is cooked, transfer it to a bowl and gradually mix in the warm coconut milk. Cover and let it absorb for 15-20 minutes.

    • 3. Saute Garlic:

  • In a large skillet or wok, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and cook until fragrant and golden, about 1-2 minutes. Be careful not to burn the garlic.

    • 4. Combine Noodles and Sauce:

  • Add the cooked noodles to the skillet and pour in the sauce mixture. Toss well to coat the noodles evenly.

    • 5. Finish and Serve:

  • Remove from heat, garnish with Parmesan cheese and scallions or parsley, and serve immediately.
