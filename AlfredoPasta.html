Creamy Alfredo Pasta Recipe

Prep Time Cook Time Total Time
10 min 0-2 min 10-12 min
Jump To Nutrition Facts
4.5(180 Reviews)

Ingredients

  • 2 large whole wheat tortillas (or your choice of wrap)
  • 1/2 cup hummus (or cream cheese, avocado spread, or yogurt-based dressing)
  • 1/2 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/2 cup sliced cucumbers
  • 1/2 cup bell peppers (red, yellow, or green), thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup spinach or mixed greens
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese (optional)
  • 1/4 cup alfalfa sprouts (optional)
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil (optional, for extra flavor)
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice (optional, for freshness)

    • Instructions

    1. Prepare the Wrap:

  • Lay the tortillas flat on a clean surface.

    • 2. Spread The Base:

  • Evenly spread hummus (or your chosen spread) over each tortilla.

    • 3. Add Vegies:

  • Layer the carrots, cucumbers, bell peppers, tomatoes, spinach, and onions.

    • 4. Optional Toppings:

  • Add feta cheese and sprouts, if using.

    • 5. Season:

  • Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice, then sprinkle with salt and pepper.

    • 6. Roll It Up:

  • Fold in the sides and tightly roll the wrap.

    • 7. Slice and Serve:

  • Cut in half and enjoy immediately, or wrap in foil for later.
