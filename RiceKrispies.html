Rice Krispies Recipe

Prep Time Cook Time Total Time
5 min 5 min 10 min (+ optional 30 mins cooling time)
Jump To Nutrition Facts
4.6 (121 Reviews)

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons (42g) unsalted butter
  • 1 package (10 oz or about 40) marshmallows
  • 6 cups Rice Krispies cereal
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt (optional, enhances flavor)

    • Instructions

    1. Melt Butter & Marshmallows:

  • In a large pot, melt the butter over low heat.
  • Add marshmallows and stir continuously until completely melted and smooth.
  • Remove from heat and stir in vanilla and salt if using.

    • 2. Mix with Cereal:

  • Gradually add Rice Krispies cereal, stirring gently until evenly coated with the marshmallow mixture.

    • 3. Press into Pan:

  • Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish with butter or non-stick spray.
  • Transfer the mixture into the dish and press it down evenly using a spatula or greased hands.

    • 4. Cool & Cut:

  • Let the treats cool at room temperature for about 30 minutes.
  • Cut into squares and enjoy!
